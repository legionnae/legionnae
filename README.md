### About Me

I'm Omar — a Computer Engineer who works across the full computing stack. From training CV and ML models in PyTorch to writing VHDL for FPGAs, from deploying inference on Jetson and edge devices to bare-metal C++ on microcontrollers. I like understanding how things work at every level.

- Currently exploring **computer vision** and **edge AI** deployment
- Building with **FPGA** and **digital design**
- Always tinkering with embedded systems — Arduino, ESP, Jetson, Pi
- Learning more about **CMOS fabrication** and **chip design**
